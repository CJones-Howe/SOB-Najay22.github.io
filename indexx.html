Origin

Yoga orginates from Indian philosophy. It started as a spiritual practice. Now many recongonize it for the promotion of mental and physical wellbeing.
Religons such as Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism have early traces of practicing yoga.

Key elements

Yoga emphasizes

-Physical postures (Asanas)
-Breathing techniques (Pranayama)
-Meditation (Dyana)

Types of Yoga

- Hatha yoga
-Yin yoga
-Iyengar yoga
-Vinyasa yoga
-Bikram yoga
-Ashtanga yoga
-Power yoga
